Restaurant review

Najdi Village

Ambience: It has a traditional, museum-like decor, which is particularly attractive to visitors.
Service: Excellent and friendly service.
Quality of food: The food is delicious and the service is excellent.

Miyazu

Ambience: Chic, modern design with dark colors and a cozy dining hall.
Service: Excellent and friendly service.
Quality of food: Known for its robata grill, sushi selection and innovative cocktails.

Coya

Ambience: Decor inspired by the Aztec civilization, with a lively atmosphere and musical tunes.
Service: Classy and professional service.
Quality of food: It offers the most delicious Latin dishes with the most delicious flavours.

Yauatcha

Zafran Indian Restaurant

Ambience: Beautiful seating and comfortable decor.

Nando's Restaurant

Ambience: Comfortable and family atmosphere.
